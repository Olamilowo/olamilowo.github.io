My favourite food is Yam and Egg sauce and i'll be giving a recipe on how to prepare it in this article

Ingredients for Yam and Egg Sauce

  • Yam
  • Egg
  • Pepper
  • vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Seasoning Cube
  • Onion

These are the ingredients needed to prepare yam and egg sauce.

How to prepare yam and egg sauce

Slice your yam into your preffered sizes and peel it off

Rinse the yam till the water is clear and has no residue

Put yam in pot and add water, add salt and cook till it is soft.

when the yam is done, drain the water out

Now that the yam is done, we proceed to making the egg saucce.

to prepare the egg sauce, we will blend/grate our pepper with onions. After blending the pepper and onion, break the eggs into a bowl, add salt and seasoning cubes then whisk the eggs until the egg whites, yolk and the spices are well mixed then set it aside.

Now you get your frying pan, put enough vegetable oil and heat for about 2 minutes then you add your blended/grated pepper and cook for 4-5 minutes. After cooking, add the whisked eggs to the pan and let it cook for a bit then you nix the egg together with the pepper.

Now that you are done, serve your yam alongside your egg sauce and enjoy your meal
