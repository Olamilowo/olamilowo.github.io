Echo Echo, It's Doctor Tennyson

When 10-year-old Ben Tennyson discovers a mysterious watch, he gains the power to transform into a number of different aliens, each with awesome powers. Ben realises that he must use these powers to help others and stop evil-doers, but that doesn't mean he's above a little super powered mischief now and then. He has a cousin Gwen and a grandfather Max Tennyson(often referred to as "grandpa Max"). Ben 10 has left a lasting impact on pop culture, influencing the superhero and action-adventure genres for children.
